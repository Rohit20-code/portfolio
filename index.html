Projects

Bidirectional Two-Way Power Converter

Designed and developed a bidirectional two-way power converter for efficient energy transfer between power sources and electrical systems. The project focused on enabling controlled two-way power flow, improving conversion efficiency and ensuring stable system performance under different operating conditions. Worked on understanding converter topology, switching techniques, voltage regulation and energy management concepts relevant to modern electrical and sustainable energy applications. The project also helped strengthen practical knowledge in power electronics, circuit analysis and real-world engineering problem solving.

Smart EV Charging Project

Developed a smart EV charging solution aimed at improving accessibility, charging efficiency and energy management for electric vehicle users. The project focused on creating a more convenient and user-friendly charging experience while addressing the growing demand for sustainable transportation systems. Worked on concepts related to smart energy utilization, charging station management and efficient power distribution. The project also explored practical approaches for optimizing charging operations and enhancing the overall usability of EV infrastructure in modern mobility applications.
